Departing

Regestration

The start time of ticket check-in and baggage check-in is determined by the airlines, this information is communicated to the passenger at the time of ticket purchase.

Estimated check-in time is 2 hours before flight departure.

Please note that some airlines increase the start time of check-in for flights:

  • TURKISH AIRLINES - 3 hours before flight departure
  • AIR CHINA - 3 hours before flight departure
  • The check-in deadline is no later than 40 minutes before the flight departure.

The exact time of the beginning and end of check-in can be found in advance at the representative offices of the airlines.

Актуальную информацию о рейсе можно получить на сайте аэропорта или в справочной службе аэропорта по телефонам:

  1. +375 17 279 13 00
  2. +375 17 279 17 30
  3. 211 (МТС, LIFE, A1)

It is recommended to arrive at the airport 2-3 hours before departure.

Passengers who are late for check-in are not allowed to fly.

If you are late for check-in, you must contact the ticket office of the relevant airline, located on the 3rd floor of the airport terminal complex, or contact the representative of the airline at the airport in the absence of the corresponding ticket office by phone, indicated on the ticket, and notify of the delay.

After check-in and baggage check-in, the passenger is directed to the screening point indicated in the boarding pass for pre-flight inspection.
