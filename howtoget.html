How to Get

In Airport

Regular buses No. 300E and 173E, fixed-route taxis No. 1400-TK, No. 1430-TK run from the National Airport to the city of Kyiv.

The official carrier is (SE) "Kyivpastrans".

You can purchase tickets for travel at the ticket offices of Kyiv bus stations, at the ticket sales terminals located at the Central bus station, on the Internet, and when departing from Kyiv National Airport - at the ticket sales terminal located in the airport building and at the stop point, from the ticket clerk at the bus stop, or from the bus driver.

The stopping point (landing place) at Kyiv National Airport is located in the area of ​​5-6 sectors (on the site of the former taxi rank, 1st floor, arrivals hall).

Travel time: about an hour.
